Name : Oxyheal
Sector : Healthtech
Team Size : 5-10
Description: A chronic wound is a wound that does not heal in an orderly set of stages and in a predictable amount of time the way most wounds do. It is claimed that over 25 billion dollars are spent worldwide annually for the treatment of chronic wounds. Even though various forms of oxygen therapy have been there for a while, there is yet to be an effective one without side effects. Also, the currently available treatments like HBOT chambers are only available in major hospitals. OxyHeal is a portable device that provides Localized Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT) to help facilitate the healing of chronic wounds.

Name : Ari Mobility Services
Sector : Micro mobility/ lifestyle/ smart transport
Team Size : 1-5
Team Pitch : link Description: Ari is a micro-mobility service operating within enclosed communities like colleges to provide an Eco-friendly, affordable and reliable solution. We build up a fleet comprising a mix of both company-owned and owner shared cycles. Owner shared cycles are added by existing cycle owners who then receive a small royalty when their cycle is used within the service. A smart lock (proprietary to Ari) is installed on every cycle. These cycles are distributed throughout the campus and can be used by anyone simply by unlocking via the app and payment is calculated per-minute of cycling. There are no fixed parking locations hence offering our customers valuable freedom to move anywhere they like within the campus. All cycles in the fleet are regularly maintained by the company.
